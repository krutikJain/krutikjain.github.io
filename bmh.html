Be My Hand

Mobile App For Disabled Students

What is Be My Hand?

Disabled students need writers for every examination, sometimes it happens that they may not fi nd out proper writer for the exams and face diffi culties. To resolve this, Be My Hand is developed which connects interested writers and students. It also has voice command-based operation. Diff erent criteria are kept for both roles for the registration, interested writers can register themselves and connect with the students.
