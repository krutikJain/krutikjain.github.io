My Dairy

Online Dairy Products Delivery App

What is My Dairy?

This Application allows customer to purchase Dairy Products Online and get it delivered early in morning which will reduce user's time, energy and efforts. Users can customize products, it will also deliver other products which are user's regular need in daily morning like bakery products, frozen items, other Dairy items like paneer, Butter, Cheese etc.
